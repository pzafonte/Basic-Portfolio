About Me

My name is Peter Zafonte. I used to be a Systems Administrator. However, I really love programming, and I quite enjoy thought of software being platform independent so that it can be accessed just about anywhere a user can log in to a browser. Therefore I decided to enter into the world of web development. I really I enjoy outdoors activities such as hiking, backpacking, kayaking, and the like. I'm also a humbly proud coffee snob.
